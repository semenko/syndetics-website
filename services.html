Services Overview

We serve to unify all features under one single umbrella, so everything — from websites, to e-mail, to social media campaigns — is branded consistently and easily updated. Unlike other providers, where these services are only offered separately and are very disjointed, we offer one single, unified solution.

  • Full Cloud Services Management
  • Online Decentralized Backup and Storage
  • Strong Web Branding
  • Unified Single Sign-On (SSO)

Integrated Cloud Services

By taking advantage of cloud infrastructure, our service infrastructure is highly reliable and secure, with an emphasis on data backups. Our systems are based on industry-standard open source software. The core components of our solutions are used throughout the web by millions of websites.

Social Media Integration

Social networks are a growing part of the internet ecosystem, and are an excellent tool to connect with a growing number of user. Our open-source content management systems can easily and seamlessly connect to a variety of social networking sites, such as Facebook, Twitter, YouTube, Scribd, and Flickr.
